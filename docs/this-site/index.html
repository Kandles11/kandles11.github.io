how did we end up here?

Updated: 2025-04-29

why does this exist?

I've always wanted to build a webpage to just store whatever I was doing. In times past, I would have created a React site, maybe Astro, and would've tried to make it look as great and "modern" as possible. After years of not having a website, I decided it was time to just throw something together and have a functional page where I can just drop whatever I please. This is my new home for thoughts, photos, projects, experiments, and anything I want to share. All you'll find here is a massive folder of html documents, and maybe some images thrown in. This is not a portfolio, this is not a demonstration of my skills, simply a place to store and share bits and pieces of my life.

ok, but how does it work?

This entire webpage was shamelessly ripped-off from a webpage I found from a YouTube video, both attached below:

  1. How DOOM Will Teach You What CS Degrees Miss (youtube.com)
  2. All You May Need is HTML (fabiensanglard.net)

After seeing this page, I was inspired, and knew I had to make something like it. With that being said, I generated a Python script with ChatGPT to make a simple generator, and created my first "article.html" (we're here now.) Paired with Inspect Element, I was able to write up some dead simple CSS, getting us to where we are today.

but whats next?

My plans going forward from this point are simple. Write more, fill the site with my favorite things, and just wait and see what happens.
